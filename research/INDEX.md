- 2026-04-01 [Genome as Spaghetti Code](2026-04-01-genome-spaghetti-code/) — The genome is measurably spaghetti code (85%+ non-functional, irremediable spliceosome complexity, megabase action-at-a-distance enhancers) but the analogy breaks in ways more interesting than where it holds: self-modification, epigenetic inheritance, and emergent evolvability have no software parallel
- 2026-04-02 [Patriarchal Ideology](2026-04-02-patriarchal-ideology/) — Every serious philosophical tradition defending patriarchy exhibits the same structural failure: the inferential bridge from "men and women differ" to "men should govern" is never successfully defended — it is assumed, smuggled in via false biology, or dependent on faith-specific premises
- 2026-04-02 [The Violence of Why-Questions](2026-04-02-why-question-violence/) — The violence of why-questions is locatable with precision in van Fraassen's contrast class: the implicit "rather than" pre-selects the explanatory space before evidence is collected, and Lewis's accommodation mechanism renders this selection invisible — demonstrated across race/IQ, poverty, gender, and the 50-year stress-ulcer misattribution in biomedicine
- 2026-04-02 [Involuntary Singlehood](2026-04-02-involuntary-singlehood/) — The singlehood crisis is a class crisis: the pool of economically viable and available non-college men halved (73% to 35%) through economic decline and selective upward poaching, while dating apps suppress commitment via evaluation-mode cognitive lock-in, and South Korea demonstrates the self-reinforcing political feedback loop where grievance-based antifeminism accelerates the marriage collapse it protests
- 2026-04-03 [Amusia and Musical Cognition](2026-04-03-amusia-and-musical-cognition/) — Congenital amusia is not a broken music module but a disconnected one: the auditory cortex detects pitch deviations the person cannot consciously perceive, the right fronto-temporal transmission pathway is structurally compromised, identical twins differing by 20,000 hours of practice show no perceptual ability difference, and the same developmental malformation produces either amusia or dyslexia depending on which hemisphere it strikes
- 2026-04-04 [Water Theory of Value](2026-04-04-water-theory-of-value/) — Shalizi's challenge (any universal input correlates with prices) is mathematically correct but empirically wrong for water: labour uniquely aligns with the Perron-Frobenius eigenvector of input-output matrices because it is the most diffusely distributed input, while the value-form school offers an entirely separate philosophical defence that Shalizi never engaged
- 2026-04-04 [Vibe Coding Blind Spots](2026-04-04-vibe-coding-blind-spots/) — Three invisible dangers for non-technical AI-assisted builders: the AI optimizes for "works" not "safe" (removing security checks that cause dev-time errors), the reading/writing distinction (side effects and idempotency), and the expert perception gap (experienced developers read structural absence, novices read textual presence)
- 2026-04-04 [LLM × Economics PhD](2026-04-04-llm-economics-phd/) — The field has institutional momentum but no unifying theoretical concept yet (unlike platform economics' cross-group externalities); three bets exist for a non-technical entrant (economics PhD requiring 8-11 years, alternative PhD at Cornell IS or Oxford OII in 4-5 years, or skip PhD for immediate industry/policy work), with the alternative PhD likely the best fit given the admissions math bar (77% of econ PhD applicants have real analysis, avg GRE quant 167.69/170) and the genuine intellectual gaps at disciplinary boundaries
- 2026-04-07 [Korinek & LLM × Econ frontier](2026-04-07-korinek-llm-econ/) — Korinek's real contribution is bridging mainstream growth theory to the AGI-scenarios / longtermist camp (the Korinek–Suh 2024 result that wages can collapse at ~80% automation is his most load-bearing theorem), while the most important news for a prospective student is not about him at all — Lindsey Raymond joining MIT in 2026 with a joint Econ/EECS/Schwarzman appointment creates an admissions window that didn't exist before, and Anders Humlum at Booth is going up for tenure around 2027, making the next 1–2 years the ideal timing to apply
- 2026-04-07 [LLM Release Cadence](2026-04-07-llm-release-cadence/) — The inflection everyone has been waiting for already happened, twice: pretraining gave way to RL post-training around April 2024, and "the model" dissolved into product around 2025 (1.3% of recent frontier-lab releases are new base weights; Anthropic ships 266 Claude Code releases for every 2 model launches) — the cadence persists not because the technology stayed constant but because the unit of release mutated each time, and the closest historical fit is Intel post-tick-tock plus Carlota Perez's installation→deployment transition
- 2026-04-08 [Four AM Waking](2026-04-08-four-am-waking/) — Clock-time 4am is a post-2015 social-media artifact (no pre-modern tradition ever specified a fixed clock hour — all were solar-relative), but by accident it is also the circadian peak of the cortisol awakening response (Bowles 2022, ~3:40am) and the hour depression colonizes involuntarily; the productivity guru and the depressive run identical hardware on opposite substrates, and the isocaloric phase-advance REM experiment that would settle the chronobiological question has never been done — the 4am ritual is a counterfeit of a multi-millennial claim ("this time is mine before the world takes it") that was severed from its solar anchor by mechanical clocks and electric light
- 2026-04-10 [Open-Weight Models](2026-04-10-open-weight-models/) — Anthropic's structural position on open-weight release is more honest than its critics give it credit for and more contingent than its defenders admit: the April 2026 Mythos Preview withholding (181 Firefox exploits vs Opus 4.6's 2; restricted to ~50 defensive partners with $100M in usage credits) is the costly action a pure rent-seeker would not take, but the broader regulatory ecosystem is on track to consolidate power in the frontier labs in exactly the bootleggers-and-baptists pattern Stigler-tradition public choice predicts (Yandle 2021 Public Choice; Gutiérrez et al. 2026 SSRN finds large AI firms gain abnormal returns on EU AI Act tightening). The empirical case is structurally a trajectory argument — RAND 2024 found null bioweapons uplift, RAND December 2025 updated itself for 2024-vintage models, Greenblatt's 100K is a Fermi estimate not a measurement — and the right reframing is coordination under irreversibility, not safety vs business
- 2026-04-11 [Anthropic: The RSP Question](2026-04-11-anthropic-one-question/) — Anthropic's Responsible Scaling Policy has never formally triggered a deployment restriction: "cyber" appears zero times in RSP v3.0, Mythos was restricted by discretionary judgment (plus compute scarcity, commercial strategy, and technical unreadiness) not a triggered safety gate, and the RSP's pause commitment was promoted from binding rule to aspirational goal while named employees acknowledged they had oversold it — but the RSP did build real organizational capacity (Frontier Red Team, 8% headcount, Constitutional Classifiers) that enabled the restriction, placing it between "elaborate cheap talk with real investment" and "early-stage norm that could harden into binding commitment," with the outcome depending on external institutional developments (EU AI Act enforcement, insurance markets, third-party auditing) rather than anything Anthropic can do unilaterally
